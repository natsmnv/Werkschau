kokofy.ioKokofy is a web-based platform that empowers anyone to create interactive videos by simply dragging, dropping, and connecting video clips into a branching narrative. It includes an intuitive Authoring Studio for creators and a lightweight Interactive Player for viewers. The project lowers the barrier to interactive media creation, allowing even non-technical users to craft immersive experiences like quizzes, clickable hotspots, and shoppable scenes, perfect for storytelling, marketing, virtual tours, and product showcasesAraz Al Hamdani
