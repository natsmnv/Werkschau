OutFeedOutfeed is an innovative digital wardrobe app that allows users to digitally catalog their clothing and make fashion decisions more consciously, creatively, and efficiently. The app is designed for anyone who wants to organize their wardrobe clearly, plan outfits, and rediscover inspiration from their own clothingÐanytime and anywhere.Felix BeerMaximilian ReiterHarald-Anton MƒllerNina BaldingerRegina MairRalitsa VelichkovaOlha DavydovaNina Baldinger
