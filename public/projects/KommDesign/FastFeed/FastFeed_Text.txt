Fast FeedThrough social networks like Instagram, TikTok and others, we are confronted with an enormous flood of information every day. Content is broken down into small, easily consumable “knowledge bites” in the form of reels and posts. The algorithm feeds us a constant stream of new input — in quantities that can never truly be digested. What remains is an unhealthy information diet that resembles the consumption of fast food: quick, convenient and instantly satisfying, yet hardly nourishing in the long run. The MMP3 project “Fast Feed” addresses this issue and makes visible just how absurd and overloaded our daily information consumption has become.
Miriam LindnerSera Alina Arld
