ChromaChroma is an immersive audiovisual VR experience exploring color
perception. Through a customizable virtual environment, users can
shape how a specific color feels to them visually and acoustically
by adjusting a range of sensory parameters. Within the installation,
participants can also experience how other users perceive the same
color, creating a direct comparison between different individual inter-
pretations. By making subjective perception tangible, Chroma high-
lights the diversity of human perception and encourages reflection
