PhysioMatePersonalized home exercises tailored to your individual needs, goals, and therapy plan help ensure effective and targeted rehabilitation. Real-time feedback via the smartphone camera enables immediate correction of movements, supporting safe and efficient training at home. On-device motion analysis provides fast, privacy-preserving evaluation directly on your smartphone without the need for cloud processing. In addition, personalized exercise videos created together with your therapist offer clear, guided instructions for each exercise. This approach increases motivation, improves adherence to the therapy program, and enhances transparency throughout the rehabilitation process.Denis SchčleChristoph Matthias Tupi
