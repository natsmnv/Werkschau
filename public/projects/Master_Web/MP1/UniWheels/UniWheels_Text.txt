Uni WheelsOur project is a shared bike platform designed exclusively for FH Salzburg students and staff. It enables users to connect with others who want to go on bike trips together, making it easier to find companions for rides, explore new routes, and stay active. The goal is to combine sport, mobility, and community by encouraging shared experiences, promoting a healthy lifestyle, and helping people build connections through cycling.Serhat SergikayaNatalia SemenovaBojidar Belov
