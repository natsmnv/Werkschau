CompulsionPlay as Milo, a snarky alien mind-parasite lodged in the brain of an anxious human named Levi. Unwelcome, uninvited, but absolutely in control... sometimes.Compulsion is a single player first-person narrative adventure focusing on free will and a struggle between individualism and collectivism. Every choice you make matters as the story unfolds in wildly different ways. Will you become human or transform humanity into something new entirely?Philip Michael EinbšckEleni KuretsidisHannah SchwaigerMarlon Sean WitteTuana YalcinHelena EbnerFlorian EberhardMatthŠus Khandare
