Scrap Defender ProtocolScrap Defender Protocol is an immersive 3D tower defense game that demands quick reflexes, keen resource management, and strategic decision-making. As you navigate the desolate wasteland, you'll need to actively mine crucial materials scattered across the map to construct an array of powerful defensive structures. The game seamlessly blends active first-person movement and survival mechanics with deep, strategic tower defense gameplay, forcing you to think on your feet as the enemy waves grow larger and more menacing.Marco HasslerLeonhard Maximilian JobstDominik LeueLinandro Valderrama
