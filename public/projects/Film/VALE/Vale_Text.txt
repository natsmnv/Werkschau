VALEVALE is a fantasy/horror short film that explores themes of guilt, self-acceptance, and queerness.The life of Janek, a forest warden carrying out his duty deep within an endless woodland, is thrown off balance by the arrival of the younger Kian. Isolated from the rest of the world, the two men have only each other.
