AquamarineDisconnected from herself and the world around her, Maris is forced to confront the emotions she has long suppressed and begins to question whether acceptance is the only way forward.

An emotional and experimental coming-of-age short film about a young person struggling with isolation and emotional repression, expressed through recurring water imagery. Through a fragmented, non-linear structure, the film explores physicality, vulnerability, and the desperate desire for human connection as inner pain slowly surfaces.
