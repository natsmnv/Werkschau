The Colors Only I Can Word is a speculative fiction project set in 2074, where genetic modification has transformed humanity. Dive into the world of Silas Mantis, the first human whose body was artificially altered and mixed with the DNA of a mantis shrimp, which left him with the ability to see colors that have never been seen before by another human, among them Pfu, Thalassan and the Twin Colours. This book invites you to imagine what lies beyond our human understanding (for now).
