“Creeplings” is a horror shortfilm in which the main character, Susan, is haunted by creepy creatures. Their origin remains a mystery, but their presence is permanent. In the end, one of them manages to touch Susan, turning her into one of their own. 
The film was made using Unreal Engine 5.6. My biggest problems came with lighting, consistency, and color grading, for which I did not allocate enough time.
